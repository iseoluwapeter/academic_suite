What Our Publications Aim to Achieve

Our publications aim to advance knowledge in computer science and technology, promote innovative solutions to campus and societal challenges, and provide actionable insights for researchers, students, and educators. Each paper highlights practical applications, methodological rigor, and contributions to the field.

An Ontology for Intra-Campus Transport System (ICTS) (A Case Study of the University of Ibadan Campus)

Oyedeji O.A
Published: 2018   |   Modified: 2023-04-14

This study looks at how to make moving around inside the University of Ibadan campus easier and better organized. It creates a smart system that understands bus routes, schedules, and stops so students and staff can get where they need to go faster and with less stress.

Efficacy of Assistive Technology for Improved Teaching and Learning in Computer Science

Enitan Olabisi Adebayo, Ibiyinka Temilola Ayorinde
Published: 2022   |   Modified: 2023-04-14

This study explored how assistive technology can improve teaching and learning in computer science within an inclusive school setting. Using a survey of students with special needs and their teachers at Durbar Grammar School, Oyo, two hypotheses were tested.
